Ministries

A Place For All Ages

Youth Ministries

The youth ministry in Malawi is a well-organized Ministry with proper structures at a national level, regional level, joint level and at a branch level. All these structures are managed by committees that comprises of the Chairperson, Vice Chairperson, secretary, vice secretary, treasurer and committee members.

Women Ministries

The Women Ministry in Malawi is a well-organized and structured ministry that focuses on empowering and supporting women in their spiritual growth, personal development, and community engagement. At the national level, the ministry is managed by a committee comprising a Chairperson, Vice Chairperson, secretary, vice secretary, treasurer, and committee members. This committee oversees and guides the ministry's activities, including organizing conferences, retreats, workshops, and seminars that address the specific needs and concerns of women.

Children Ministries

The Children Ministry in Malawi is a well-structured and organized ministry dedicated to the spiritual, emotional, and social development of children. They also foster an inclusive and nurturing environment for children to learn and grow in their faith, character, and relationships. The Children Ministry's committee at each level works together to provide guidance, resources, and support for the branches, ensuring that children across Malawi have access to quality spiritual education and opportunities for personal development.

Ministry Events

Youth Camp

The Youth Camps are conducted by both the regional committees and the National committees on interchanging arrangements. Thus, the nation Youth camps every two years and in those years the regions do not conduct theirs. The National youth camps are done in the Lake Malawi districts to give the youth access to beautiful beaches as part of recreation. These Youth Camps take a period of Six days from Sunday to Saturday.

This brief introduces the Central Africa Conference (CAC) of the Seventh Day Baptist, covering its background, organization, current successes, and challenges. The CAC operates in Malawi and parts of Mozambique, with its headquarters in Malawi. It has a predominantly Christian population, and the majority of Sabbath keepers are Seventh Day Adventists (SDA) and Seventh Day Baptists (SDB).
