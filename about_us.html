Our Beliefs

Seventh Day Baptists consider liberty of thought under the guidance of the Holy Spirit to be essential to Christian belief and practice. Therefore we encourage the unhindered study and open discussion of Scripture. We uphold the individual’s freedom of conscience in seeking to determine and obey the will of God. The following statement is not intended to be exhaustive, but is an expression of our common belief, which is derived from our understanding of Scripture. 2 Corinthians 3:17-18; 2 Timothy 2:15; Romans 12:2; Ephesians 4:3-6, 15; Romans 10:17; 2 Timothy 3:16-17

Our Vision

Welcome to Malawi, the warm heart of Africa. This brief introduces the Central Africa Conference (CAC) of the Seventh Day Baptist, covering its background, organization, current successes, and challenges. The CAC operates in Malawi and parts of Mozambique, with its headquarters in Malawi. It has a predominantly Christian population, and the majority of Sabbath keepers are Seventh Day Adventists (SDA) and Seventh Day Baptists (SDB).

Our Mission

Our mission as a church comes from the Great Commission of Jesus Christ who commanded us to “go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you.” (Matthew 28: 16-20)
